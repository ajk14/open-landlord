About the Landlord Report Card

The Landlord Report Card is a volunteer-led, grassroots project based in Albany, NY, working to provide greater transparency into the City's data for tenants across the city. It is an open-source project whose code is avaible here: https://github.com/ajk14/open-landlord

Contact Us

If you have feedback on the site or wish to get into contact with the developers and maintainers of the site, you can email us at info@albanylandlord.com
